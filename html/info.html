About

The Clouded project refers to a cloud storage service designed for students. With our software, we aim to provide students with a modern and secure platform where they can conveniently store, organize, and access their learning materials and personal documents.

Project Context

As part of the curriculum at the HTBLVA Wien 5 Spengergasse, the Clouded project is developed within the framework of the Project Development (PRE) subject. With the directive from the teaching authority, Mag. Michael Dolezal, the Simat Solutions™ team planned and ultimately commissioned this project idea.

Goals

As providers of the cloud storage service, we have clear goals that impact both the efficiency of our software and customer satisfaction.

  • I. Security: Data security is of utmost priority. The cloud storage should adhere to the highest security standards to safeguard the privacy of users.

  • II. User-Friendliness: Our goal is to create a user-friendly interface that is easy to navigate. We aim to ensure that users can access their data easily and effectively.

  • III. Innovation: We aspire to develop an innovative platform that aligns with the requirements of digital education, providing users with a modern approach to accessing learning materials.
